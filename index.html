The Bishop's Science Society

Science Club is an afterschool, mentor-based science program . This program brings graduate student mentors and science enthusiasts together with small groups of students to conduct fun and engaging scientific investigations. Learn more about us here.

About

We engage students in ways that can be transformational and inspire them to be young scientists!
Science can be fun when students get to understand different topics by:

  • Science experiments
  • Science discussions
  • Model making
  • Audiovisuals
  • Science worksheets
  • Quiz sessions
